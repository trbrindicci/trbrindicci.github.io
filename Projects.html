Who Can Replace 
Mbappé?

French superstar Kylian Mbappé is rumored to leave PSG after Messi's arrival. I have conducted a K-Nearest Neighbor analysis in order to see how can the club replace him, by finding players that performed statistically similar to him in the Top 5 ​Leagues in Europe, in the season 2020-2021

COVID-19 Vaccine
Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment Analysis is done by Classifying the Positive tweets from negative tweets by machine learning models for classification, text mining, text analysis, data analysis and data visualization. 

The objective of this project was to quantitatively analyze the people’s reaction to COVID-19 Vaccine on Twitter and get information on the way they think and feel about it, and what other topics are related to their feelings.
